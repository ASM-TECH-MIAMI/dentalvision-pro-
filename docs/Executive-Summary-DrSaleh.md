# DENTALVISION PRO — Executive Summary

**AI Smile Design Studio for Ora Dentistry Spa**

---

## What Is It?

DentalVision Pro is a private, AI-powered platform that transforms your smile design workflow. It captures, analyzes, simulates, and documents — all in one branded system that runs on-premises in your practice.

## The Core Problem

Today's cosmetic dental workflow is fragmented: generic video calls for virtual consults, hours of manual CAD sculpting per case, subjective shade matching, scattered documentation, and patients who wait days to see what their smile could look like.

## What Changes

| | Today | With DentalVision Pro |
|---|---|---|
| **First smile preview** | Days to weeks | Seconds — during the consultation |
| **Virtual consults** | FaceTime/WhatsApp, no dental intelligence | Branded platform with live AI analysis |
| **Design starting point** | Blank CAD canvas, manual sculpting | AI-generated proposal, you refine |
| **Shade matching** | Physical guide, variable lighting | Calibrated digital VITA matching |
| **Documentation** | Manual, 15-30 min per case | Automatic — every case archived |
| **Patient data** | Scattered across cloud services | On-premises, structured, private |

## How It Works

**Visit 1 (Consultation):** Guided photo capture, instant AI analysis (478 facial landmarks, teeth detection, shade matching), real-time smile simulation, auto-generated treatment plan.

**Visit 2 (Design Review):** AI-proposed design, interactive refinement with patient present, AR live preview via webcam, preparation guide generation.

**Visit 3 (Final Placement):** Calibrated shade verification, automatic before/after documentation, complete case archive, patient smile portfolio.

**Virtual Consults:** Browser-based (no app install), AI overlay on live video, auto-capture, instant treatment plan after the call.

## Built for Your Brand

Deep black, warm gold, soft cream. Playfair Display headings. Photography-first layouts. The same before/after slider from your gallery. Not generic dental software — an extension of Ora Dentistry Spa.

## Technology

- AI runs locally — patient data never leaves your office
- Mac Mini server, no cloud dependency
- MediaPipe for facial analysis, SAM2 for teeth segmentation
- Real-time WebRTC for virtual consultations
