## THE PROBLEM TODAY

### How Smile Design Currently Works

Today's cosmetic dental workflow relies on a chain of disconnected tools and manual processes:

**Consultation**
- Virtual consultations happen over FaceTime, Google Duo, or WhatsApp — generic platforms with zero dental intelligence
- Photos are taken separately, emailed, and manually organized
- Shade selection relies on physical VITA guides under variable lighting
- Treatment plans are written by hand and sent after the visit

**Design**
- CAD software like Ceramill Mind requires a skilled technician to sculpt each tooth restoration individually using free-form brushes — a process that takes significant time per case
- Each tooth's anatomy (mammelons, developmental lobes, surface texture) must be manually shaped
- Surgical guides and frameworks are designed separately
- The virtual try-in (overlaying the design on the patient's photo) is a manual compositing step done at the end

**Communication**
- Patients wait days or weeks to see what their new smile will look like
- There is no way to show a realistic preview during the initial consultation
- Before/after comparisons require manual photo editing
- Case documentation is scattered across files, folders, and systems

### The Cost of Manual Workflows

| Challenge | Impact |
|---|---|
| Manual CAD sculpting per tooth | Hours of skilled technician time per case |
| No instant simulation at consultation | Patient hesitation, delayed case acceptance |
| Generic video call platforms | No clinical capture, no AI assistance during call |
| Disconnected photo management | Lost images, inconsistent documentation |
| Manual treatment plan writing | Administrative burden, potential for errors |
| No standardized shade matching | Subjective results under variable lighting |

## THE SOLUTION

### DentalVision Pro — One Platform, Every Step

DentalVision Pro digitizes your entire smile design workflow into a single, branded platform. AI handles the heavy lifting — analysis, initial design proposals, shade matching, documentation — while you retain full creative control to refine every detail.

### How It Works: Your Three-Visit Workflow, Elevated

---

### VISIT 1 — CONSULTATION

**Today:** Take photos manually. Evaluate by eye. Write notes. Email a plan later.

**With DentalVision Pro:**

1. **Smart Capture** — A guided photo wizard ensures clinical-grade images every time. The system detects face position, lighting, and smile angle, capturing automatically when conditions are optimal.

2. **Instant AI Analysis** — Within seconds of capture:
   - 478 facial landmarks mapped for proportion analysis
   - Every visible tooth detected and segmented automatically
   - VITA shade matched against the Classical scale using calibrated color science
   - Facial proportions evaluated: golden ratio, smile arc, buccal corridor width
   - Dental notation auto-labeled (FDI Universal system)

3. **Live Smile Simulation** — The patient sees a realistic preview of their new smile overlaid on their own photo — during the consultation, not weeks later. Adjustable parameters let you explore different shapes, shades, and proportions together.

4. **Auto-Generated Treatment Plan** — Based on the analysis, the system produces a branded treatment plan with fee estimates, ready to present or email immediately.

**Result:** Patients see their future smile in the first visit. Case acceptance increases dramatically.

---

### VISIT 2 — DESIGN REVIEW

**Today:** Review the CAD technician's work. Iterate. Send revisions back and forth.

**With DentalVision Pro:**

1. **AI-Proposed Design** — Instead of starting from a blank canvas in CAD software, the system generates an initial tooth design proposal based on the patient's facial analysis, proportions, and your selected parameters.

2. **Interactive Refinement** — With the patient present, adjust tooth shape, shade, length, width, and gum line using intuitive sliders. Changes render in real-time.

3. **AR Live Preview** — Using the patient's webcam, see the proposed design overlaid on their face in real-time as they move and smile naturally.

4. **Preparation Guide** — The system generates a minimum-reduction guide per tooth, displayed as a color-coded depth map — ensuring conservative preparation aligned with your no-prep veneer philosophy.

**Result:** Design review becomes a collaborative, real-time experience with the patient, not a back-and-forth with a lab.

---

### VISIT 3 — FINAL PLACEMENT

**Today:** Manual shade verification. Photos for records. Case closed without structured archive.

**With DentalVision Pro:**

1. **Calibrated Shade Verification** — Compare the final restoration shade against the original analysis using calibrated photography, eliminating guesswork.

2. **Before/After Documentation** — Automatic generation of your signature before/after comparison — the same draggable slider presentation from your gallery, branded to Ora Dentistry Spa.

3. **Complete Case Archive** — Every photo, analysis, modification, and consultation note is stored in a structured digital timeline. Nothing is lost. Every case is retrievable.

4. **Patient Smile Portfolio** — A shareable digital portfolio the patient can access — perfect for referrals and social proof.

**Result:** Every case is documented to the highest standard, automatically.

## VIRTUAL CONSULTATIONS — REIMAGINED

### Beyond FaceTime

Your current virtual consultations work — but they could work harder. DentalVision Pro replaces generic video calls with a purpose-built dental consultation platform.

**How It Works:**

1. Patient receives a branded link (no app install required)
2. Browser opens with camera — a guided overlay helps them position correctly
3. AI face tracking begins automatically, detecting smiles and optimal capture angles
4. You see the patient's live video feed WITH AI overlay: landmarks, teeth regions, shade analysis
5. You can freeze any frame, run a full analysis, and show the smile simulation — live during the call
6. After the call: treatment plan with captured photos and analysis is auto-generated and emailed

**For the Patient:**
- No app to download — just click a link
- Guided experience — the system tells them how to position for the best capture
- They see their simulation during the call — not in a follow-up email days later

**For You:**
- Every virtual consult produces clinical-grade data automatically
- AI runs in real-time — you see what the algorithms see
- Treatment plans generated immediately after the call
- Full recording available for case documentation (with consent)

## THE TECHNOLOGY

### Built for Privacy, Speed, and Precision

| Capability | Technology | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| Face Analysis | MediaPipe (478 landmarks) | Runs in the browser — instant, no upload needed |
| Teeth Segmentation | SAM2 (Meta's Segment Anything) | Precise tooth-by-tooth isolation for analysis |
| Shade Matching | Calibrated color science | VITA Classical scale matching, not subjective guessing |
| Smile Simulation | AI-generated overlay | Realistic preview on the patient's actual photo |
| 3D Visualization | React Three Fiber | Interactive 3D dental models in the browser |
| Virtual Consult | WebRTC (real-time video) | Peer-to-peer, low latency, browser-based |
| Data Storage | PostgreSQL database | Structured, relational, queryable patient records |

### On-Premises — Your Data Stays in Your Practice

DentalVision Pro runs on a Mac Mini server in your office. Patient photos, X-rays, analyses, and case data never leave your premises. No cloud dependency. No third-party access. Full HIPAA-aligned data control.
